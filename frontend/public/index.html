About GreenLoop

GreenLoop leverages blockchain technology to reduce single-use plastic by promoting reusable packaging and tracking its entire lifecycle. Our platform ensures transparency and accountability, encouraging consumers and businesses to make sustainable choices.

Founded by a team of passionate developers from Chandigarh University, GreenLoop aims to revolutionize retail packaging through blockchain-backed traceability and smart incentives. Our solution empowers consumers to participate in the circular economy while holding retailers accountable for their environmental impact.

Through our platform, consumers can scan packaging QR codes to access detailed information on its origin, usage history, and eco-impact. We automate rewards through smart contracts and encourage sustainable behavior with eco-credits redeemable for discounts or donations.

Join us on our mission to create a plastic-free retail ecosystem. Whether you are a business looking to switch to sustainable packaging or a consumer striving to reduce your carbon footprint, GreenLoop offers the tools and incentives needed for a greener future.
